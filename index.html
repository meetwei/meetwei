Lynk Answers

Knowledge Management Solution

Lynk Answers is an AI/ML expert network that provides businesses with on-demand access to insights and expertise from a global network of highly skilled professionals. The platform offers customized research, expert consultations, and expert network services to clients in various industries, including finance, consulting, and technology. By connecting clients with the right experts, Lynk Answers helps businesses make informed decisions and stay ahead of the competition.

As the Lead Product Manager, I was responsible for developing and executing the product roadmap for Lynk Answer, resulting in the attainment of 1 billion highly skilled experts in the talent pool by the end of 2022. I also managed the product operations.

  • Client: Consulting Firms, Financial Institutions, Law Firms, and Corporations across various industries
  • Tech Stack: MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, NodeJS), Python, Django, Postgres, AWS (EC2, S3, RDS), Docker, Kubernetes, and OpenSearch
